Whack-a-mole

Game Overview:

The game is a Whack-a-Mole variant where players must click on moles as they randomly appear in holes. The player's objective is to hit as many moles as possible within a given time frame. Moles spawn randomly, and players must react quickly to click on them before they disappear. The game ends after a certain duration, and players' performance is evaluated based on their score, accuracy, and reaction time.

Game Parameters:

Score: Tracks the player's performance by counting successful hits on moles.

Accuracy: Measures the player's precision in hitting the moles, calculated as the percentage of successful hits out of total attempts.

Average reaction Time: Evaluates the player's responsiveness by calculating the average time taken to hit the moles after they appear.

Duration: Records the total time spent playing the game in each session.
